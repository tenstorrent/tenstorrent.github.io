Tenstorrent Docs — How to view in your browser
==============================================

1. Unzip this folder anywhere on your computer.

2. Open index.html in your web browser (double-click it, or drag it into Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.).
   That is the docs home page.

3. To go directly to the TT-QuietBox 2 (Blackhole) docs:
   - Open the folder: systems → quietbox → quietbox-bh-2
   - Double-click index.html (overview), setup.html (setup guide), or specifications.html (specs)

All formatting, images, and links work offline. No server or internet needed.
